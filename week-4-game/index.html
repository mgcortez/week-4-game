Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below, click on a crystal to add points to your total score. You win by matching your total score with a random generated number. Go above that random number and you lose! The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it. The value of each crystal will change each time the game restarts. Good luck!
